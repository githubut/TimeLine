2000-2026 AD: Contemporary Era

The 21st century begins with digital connectivity and proceeds through pivotal moments that shape our current world.

Major Events

Millennium and Y2K (2000 AD)

World celebrates the year 2000. Fears of computer crashes from Y2K bug prove largely unfounded.

9/11 Terrorist Attacks (2001 AD)

Al-Qaeda terrorists attack USA, killing nearly 3,000 people. This event shapes global politics, security, and military strategy for decades.

Iraq and Afghanistan Wars (2001-2021 AD)

US-led wars in Middle East last two decades, creating instability and regional conflicts.

Financial Crisis (2008 AD)

Global economic collapse causes recession. Banks fail, unemployment rises, and governments implement massive bailouts.

COVID-19 Pandemic (2019-2026 AD)

Global pandemic kills over 6-7 million people. Lockdowns, vaccines, and remote work transform society and economy.

Artificial Intelligence Boom (2010s-2026 AD)

AI and machine learning advance rapidly. Large language models like ChatGPT emerge, transforming technology and raising ethical questions.

Global Challenges & Developments

Climate Change Crisis

Global temperatures rise due to greenhouse gas emissions. Extreme weather, rising sea levels, and biodiversity loss accelerate.

Social Media Revolution

Platforms like Facebook, Twitter, and TikTok connect billions globally, transforming communication but also spreading misinformation.

Energy Transition

Renewable energy, electric vehicles, and green initiatives become mainstream responses to climate and energy challenges.

Geopolitical Tensions

Rising tensions between major powers including US, China, and Russia reshape global alliances and international relations.
